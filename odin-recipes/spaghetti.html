Spaghetti

Description

Ingredients

  • Pasta noodles
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Sausage
  • Ground Beef
  • Garlic
    •

    Steps

    1. Boil the pasta in water for 10 minutes
    2. Heat up ground beef and sausage for 5 minutes
    3. Pour sauce over the meat
    4. Drain the noodles and mix with sauce
