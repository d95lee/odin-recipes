Pizza

Description:

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Sausage
  • Tomato Sauce
    •

    Steps

    1. Roll out the dough into a flat circular shape
    2. Pour tomato sauce on top
    3. Sprinkle cheese over the top
    4. Sprinkle the rest of the ingredients over the top
    5. Finally put the end product in the oven and allow to sit for 30 minutes
